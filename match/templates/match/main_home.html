Ottyr


Finding stepping stones to the stars.


Making quality matches isn't just for dating anymore.

At Ottyr, we are committed to changing the definition of finding mentorships from searching for question-answering guides to making connections that inspire. We are committed to breaking the barriers that currently stand in the way of finding the right mentor in college.

In place of manually-matched pairs with little follow-up, we use cutting-edge algorithms and technology to connect people with their future mentors and mentees, creating friendships that make a difference and relationships that matter. No more “hi”-only mentors, awkward approaches, or lost time that could be used making a difference in a student’s career. During their college experience, students who find mentors or mentees can find themselves stressed less over issues that are extremely detrimental academically, physically, and mentally.

  • improve community integration
